 Personality Test Results

An ISTP (Introverted, Sensing, Thinking, Perceiving) is someone with the Introverted, Observant, Thinking, and Prospecting personality traits. They tend to have an individualistic mindset, pursuing goals without needing much external connection. They engage in life with inquisitiveness and personal skill, varying their approach as needed. As someone who loves action, new experiences, hands-one activities and the freedom to work at my own pace, this personalty type applies to me.
